What we do currently:

Bi-Weekly Meetings

We gather every other fridays if we need to.

League of Legend Competitive Matches

We currently have six teams on rotations playing competitively within our school. We are hoping to add livestreaming to our matches in the future.

Super Smash Bro Tournaments

We hold monthly tournaments for Super Smash Brothers within the school.

We're Hiring!

We are looking for awesome people to join our crew.

Our Positions:

Content Creators

We are looking for creative people who would like to create gaming content in either: live-streaming, video, audio or written formats. We would be supporting the use of Twitch, YouTube, SoundCloud and Medium as our publishing platform.

Social Media Coordinators

We are looking for people that wants to help connect the Vaughan Community and the public to our gaming club. We will be using both Twitter and Instagram to help develop an online presence for the club.

Event Coordinators

We are looking for people who are interested in organizing various events in the name of gaming club. The events can include interclub meetings, external tournaments or fundraisers for the club or a NPO.

Game Managers

We are looking for people or groups of people to represent their favourite game. This will be an unique position because one person would not be able to represent multiple games and the position has a strict application process.
